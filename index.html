Pascal Delaquis is a drummer from Canada with a passion for percussive textures and deep grooves. He learned on pots and pans; a practice that has evolved into his present-day inquiries in tone and sound. He has collaborated on acclaimed studio albums, including Little Scream's Speed Queen (Merge Records). On the road, Pascal has performed extensive touring throughout the Americas and Europe with the highly acclaimed Elisapie during the album cycle of The Ballad of the Runaway Girl.
