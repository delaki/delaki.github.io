Pascal Delaquis
is a drummer, composer, and producer from Canada. He learned on pots and pans; a practice that has evolved into his present-day inquiries in tone and sound. He has collaborated on acclaimed studio albums, including Little Scream's Speed Queen (Merge Records). On the road, Pascal has toured internationally with Juno Award-winning project Elisapie.
